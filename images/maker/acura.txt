Acura is the American luxury vehicle marque of Japanese automaker Honda. The brand was launched in the United States and Canada on 27 March 1986, marketing luxury, performance, and high-performance vehicles. Acura holds the distinction of being the first American-Japanese automotive luxury brand. It is no coincidence that the Acura receives high ratings for safety on the highway. Not only will Acura get your family to their destination with comfort and speed, it will also offer peace of mind knowing that it has all the safety features that protects the riders if anything unexpected was to take place.
