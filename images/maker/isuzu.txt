Isuzu Motors Ltd  is a Japanese commercial vehicle and diesel engine manufacturing company headquartered in Tokyo. Its principal activity is the production, marketing and sale of Isuzu commercial vehicles and diesel engines. Isuzu's primary market focus is on commercial diesel-powered truck, buses and construction, while their Japanese competitor Yanmar focuses on commercial-level powerplants and generators.  Isuzu is well known for their truck based vehicles and their more popular in Asian and African continents too. But in the 1990s Isuzu has collapsed on the sales of their small level sedans and cars.
