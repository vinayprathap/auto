JAGUAR  is the luxury vehicle brand of Jaguar Land Rover,[2][1] a British multinational car manufacturer with its headquarters in Whitley, Coventry, England. Jaguar Cars was the company that was responsible for the production of Jaguar cars until its operations were fully merged with those of Land Rover to form Jaguar Land Rover on 1 January 2013. The Jaguar brand was getting popular after they manufactured and marketed the sports car models like Jaguar XK120, Jaguar XK 140 and other few in the periods of 1950s. for such long years Jaguar was suing the slogan called “Grace, Space, Pace” and they have achieved records in their sales during they were using this slogan. In 1951 and in 1953, these were the years the Jaguar model sports cars were won the championship of the Le Mans 24 Hour Race and those were real delightful moments in their long history. We can say the X models and the R models are the stylish products of Jaguar till now. Under the product of luxury Jaguar has released their Jaguar KX in 2006 and its structure has made by aluminium and it was a two door vehicle with fixed roof. In 1990 Ford acquired Jaguar Cars and it remained in their ownership, joined in 2000 by Land Rover, till 2008. Ford then sold both Jaguar and Land Rover to Tata Motors. Tata created Jaguar Land Rover as a subsidiary holding company. At operating company level, in 2013 Jaguar Cars was merged with Land Rover to form Jaguar Land Rover Limited as the single design, manufacture, sales company and brand owner for both Jaguar and Land Rover vehicles.
