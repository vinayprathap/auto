TYPE -  USED OEM PART
CATEGORY - DRIVESHAFT AND AXEL
LOCATION - USUALLY NEAR THE WHEELS
INCLUDES - SPINDLE KNUCKLE ONLY

INFO -  It is variously called a steering knuckle, spindle, upright or hub, as well. The wheel and tire assembly attach to the hub or spindle of the knuckle where the tire/wheel rotates while being held in a stable plane of motion by the knuckle/suspension assembly.
